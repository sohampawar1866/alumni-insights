# Alumni Insights Design System

## Theme: Neo-Brutalism (Gumroad Style)

**Atmosphere & Visual Language:**
- **Vibe:** Neo-brutalist, playful, bold, high-contrast.
- **Key Elements:** Hard solid black borders on cards, buttons, and form inputs. Solid blocky shadows (`box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px #000` instead of soft blurred dropshadows).

**Color Palette:**
- **Backgrounds:** Stark white (`#ffffff`) or a warm cream/off-white (`#fcfaf6`).
- **Primary Accents:** Bright pop colors (Lime Green `#00e559`, Cyan `#2196f3`, or Vivid Yellow `#ffc900`). These should be used for call-to-action buttons, badges, and highlights.
- **Text & Borders:** Jet Black (`#000000`).

**Typography:**
- Oversized, heavy headings (bold/black weights).
- Clean geometric sans-serif for body.

**Shapes & Layout:**
- **Corners:** Sharp (0px) or slightly rounded (`4px` to `8px`) but ALWAYS paired with thick solid borders (e.g. `border-2 border-black` or `border-[3px] border-black`).
- **Layout:** Sectioned with thick horizontal/vertical divider lines, grid-based, high contrast.
